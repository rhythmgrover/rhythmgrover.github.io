Rhythm Grover

I am an Assistant Professor in the Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. My research focuses on developing statistical methodology for modeling non-stationary signals. This field of research is applicable to solve problems in diverse fields of science and engineering such as environmental sciences, communication systems, biomedical, acoustics, finance, and stock valuation, image processing, and others.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Indian Statistical Institute Delhi in the Theoretical Statistics and Mathematics Unit, advised by Prof. Swagata Nandi. I was a former Ph.D. student at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics with Prof. Prof. Debasis Kundu and Prof. Amit Mitra .
